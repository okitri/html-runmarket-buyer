Attention

Before importing or inserting seller information into the RUN Market Platform, please read the conditions below :
  • By inserting or importing Seller Information data, your company indirectly registers your company's vendor to the RUN Market Platform to become a seller.
  • After successfully inserting or importing Seller Information data, RUN Market will send an Email Verification to your company’s vendor. Email Verification must be responded by your company's vendor within 24 hours.
  • If the Email Verification is not responded by your company's vendor within 24 hours, then the data that has been inserted or imported will not appear in the Seller Information. We suggest that your company can make announcements to your company's vendors who will be inserted or imported and become Sellers on the RUN Market Platform.
